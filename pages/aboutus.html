Why Join Sam's English School?


At Sam's English School, we are constantly on the lookout for talented and passionate educators to join our dynamic team. We believe that our teachers are the cornerstone of our success, providing high-quality education and inspiring our students to achieve their full potential.



We Are Seeking Native English Speakers


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Native English Speaker: Fluency in English is a must, as we strive to offer authentic language experiences to our students.
  • Bachelor's Degree in Education: A strong educational background is key to understanding and implementing effective teaching methodologies.
  • Teaching Certification: We value teachers who are formally certified and have a deep understanding of pedagogical techniques.
  • Minimum of Two Years of Experience: Experience matters. We look for teachers who have honed their skills in the classroom and can bring real-world insights to our students.
  • CELTA Certification: Teachers with CELTA certification will be given preference, as this demonstrates a commitment to teaching English as a second language.

How to Apply


If you meet the above qualifications and are excited about the prospect of contributing to a vibrant learning community, we would love to hear from you.


Application Process:

  1. Prepare your CV and a cover letter detailing your teaching philosophy and experience.
  2. Gather copies of your relevant certifications and qualifications.
  3. Compile a portfolio of your teaching materials or lesson plans (if available).

Submission:

Please send all application materials to sam@samenglishschool.com. Ensure that your documents are well-organized and clearly labeled.

Why Choose Sam's English School?


Joining Sam's English School means becoming part of a supportive and innovative educational environment. We offer competitive salaries, professional development opportunities, and a chance to make a significant impact in the lives of our students.

We look forward to reviewing your application and potentially welcoming you to our team!
